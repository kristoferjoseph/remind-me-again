Remind me again…

  • Be your best self

    We are all a work in progress. It is too easy to hate on myself for imperfections in the past. Every day is a new day. Strive to be a better you, forgive your past self and move forward.

  • Empathy is the most powerful tool.

    Understand people’s motivations and what they aspire to in order to serve them better.

  • Fail harder

    Not everything works, but that doesn’t mean that the next attempt starts at zero. All endeavors inform the next. Never ever be afraid to try.

  • Question assumptions

    There is always room for improvment. Just because it has always been done some way does not mean it is the best, just the most familiar.

  • Do the impossible

    Doubt is natural, but not productive.

  • Iterate, iterate, iterate.

    Nothing is perfect, but constant iteration get’s us constantly closer.

  • Use your product.

    To truly understand your users you need to feel their triumphs and their pains.

  • Take time to acknowledge accomplishments.

    Everyone has their own strengths and their own challenges. It is too easy to criticize. It is much harder to genuinely recognize a person’s individual growth and success.
